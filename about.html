More About Me

I am a senior at Northeastern University majoring in Finance and minoring in Data Science. I enjoy combining quantitative thinking with creativity, and I am especially interested in how data and analytics can be used to understand people, markets, and behavior. Over the past few years, I have taken classes that span investing, statistics, and data visualization, and I am excited to keep building these skills as I move into my career.

Music has always been a huge part of my life. I started playing piano and guitar when I was five years old and have continued performing and creating music ever since. At Northeastern, I helped start a small music production business where we hosted events in Boston and mixed music live. I also love staying active and taking workout classes. Right now, I work at a pilates studio on Newbury Street, which lets me combine my interest in fitness with a fun, community‑oriented job.

Some things I enjoy

  • Going to workout and pilates classes
  • Exploring Boston with friends
